## What is OpenCalc?

OpenCalc is a lightweight, open-source calculator for Android. Built for users who want a fast, privacy-respecting calculator without unnecessary bloat, it covers everything from everyday arithmetic to scientific functions — all in an APK under 2 MB.

It has been downloaded more than 300,000 times across Google Play, F-Droid, IzzyOnDroid, and GitHub, and is actively maintained by the open-source community.

## Requirements

- **Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later** — `minSdk 21`
- **Version:** 3.2.1 (`versionCode` 54)

OpenCalc targets Android 14 (SDK 35) and is compiled with Kotlin on the JVM. It runs on any Android device released from 2014 onward.

## App permissions

OpenCalc requests only the permissions listed in its manifest:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `VIBRATE` | Haptic feedback when tapping calculator buttons. |
| `SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW` | Enables the app to display on the lock screen. |
| `USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT` | Supports lock screen display and quick settings tile. |

There is no `INTERNET` permission. OpenCalc never sends data off your device.
